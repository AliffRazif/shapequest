SHAPEQUEST

You have been transported to the dimension of shapes by the evil shape demon Ahriman( 𐬀𐬢𐬭𐬀⸱𐬨𐬀𐬌𐬥𐬌𐬌𐬎 ) and have been cursed and turned into a red vertical rectangle! In order to escape you must absorb enough yellow circles or you will loose any semblance of consciousness and will remain in the dimension of shapes for eternity! Remember in this world the circles are your friends, the light green circles have healing properties and will help to heal you, anything else there will being harm to you. So avoid them!! Press the spacebar to jump over anything that is not a circle!

Jump: Spacebar

Doublejump: Double tap spacebar
